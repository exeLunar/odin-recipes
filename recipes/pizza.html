Handmade Pepperoni Pizza

This delicious pizza is made with our Homemade dough and topped with our secret Tomato Sauce, fresh Mozzarella Cheese, and Pepperoni

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Pepperoni
  • Secret Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Add flour to your table
  2. Place the dough and begin to roll it out until flat
  3. Add your Tomato Sauce
  4. Add your Toppings
  5. Bake in the oven at 350 for 20 minutes
